FIRST IDEA

For the animation theme we had to create a game. First thing that came to my mind was a jumping star game. For somebody like me, who wants to be a game developer, this theme was the best one yet. I was hyped up and I started sketching the beginning of the game and I also came up with the story plot. Click here if you want to see the full plot, but basically it is about two stars that fell on Earth and now they need to go back in space.

DIAGRAM

After I finished the sketch I draw a diagram of the game. I needed to know what happens when the game starts. I needed to know when is the player winning or losing. This is why I had to create a diagram.

I learnt that everything has to have a diagram. It is necessary for the developer so he knows what are the steps that the user can make.
