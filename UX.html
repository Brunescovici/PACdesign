INTERVIEW RESEARCH

In this part of the UX theme I knew it gets a bit complicated. I learnt how to manage the time for an interview, how to be friendly but in the same time not waste much time on little discussions.

SURVEY RESEARCH

The survey research opened my mind. Sometimes it is good to listen what others have to say. I learnt that if you are doing something just for yourself, you are targeting a small audience. It is good to take some tips from others and make your project for everyone!

In this one I also learnt how to kindly share your survey to others and how to create them in order for the one filling the form to actually think about it.
